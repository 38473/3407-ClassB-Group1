About us

About MyClean

MyClean is an intelligent cleaning service platform designed for modern urban residents and cleaning service providers. It is committed to reshaping the housekeeping and cleaning service industry through technological innovation and excellent user experience. Whether you are a busy professional, a housewife, or a professional cleaning company, MyClean can provide you with an efficient, safe and transparent service experience.


Cleaning Services We Offer

we are committed to making your space spotless and stress-free. We offer a wide range of professional cleaning services tailored to your needs:

  • ✓ Home Cleaning – Regular or one-time cleaning for apartments, condos, and houses.
  • ✓ Deep Cleaning – Thorough top-to-bottom cleaning for kitchens, bathrooms, and other high-use areas.
  • ✓ Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning – Ensure a fresh start or smooth transition with detailed property cleaning.
  • ✓ Office & Commercial Cleaning – Keep your workplace clean, healthy, and professional.
  • ✓ Pet-Friendly Cleaning – Specialized care for homes with furry friends.
  • ✓ Custom Packages & Subscriptions – Flexible cleaning plans to fit your schedule and budget.

1. Intelligent positioning recommendation system
When users make an appointment, their location information is automatically collected. The platform will recommend the best nearby cleaning service personnel based on distance, service quality and available time, saving communication costs and improving service efficiency.

2. Intelligent customer service and real-time chat system
Built-in human-machine hybrid intelligent customer service system. Users can communicate with cleaning staff in real time through the website. When the other party is offline, the AI ​​chat robot automatically connects to provide users with quick question-answering services. When encountering complex problems, the system can seamlessly transfer to manual customer service or cleaning staff.

3. Multi-language real-time translation support
The platform supports communication in multiple languages. Whether it is between users and chat robots or real customer service, the content can be automatically translated, making cross-cultural communication barrier-free and improving the global accessibility and inclusiveness of the platform.

4. Appointment and scheduling management system
Cleaning companies or individual service providers can manage customer appointment information, work arrangements and customer needs through exclusive backends, thereby achieving more efficient business operations.

5. User feedback and platform optimization mechanism
MyClean encourages users to provide feedback on their service experience and report problems at any time. The platform will handle them in a timely manner and continuously optimize algorithms and operational services to form a virtuous circle.

6. Chat history backtracking function
During the conversation between users and customer service or service personnel, all conversations will be recorded in the chat history, which is convenient for subsequent viewing and reference, and improves service continuity.

7. Diversified payment methods and refund mechanism
Users can choose online payment (credit card, PayPal) or offline payment in the application. The system will generate an electronic invoice and support users to quickly apply for a refund after canceling the order, making the transaction safe, transparent, flexible and convenient.

Our Vision

We hope to improve the overall standards of the cleaning service industry through the combination of technology and humanistic design, provide users with an easy, time-saving and safe service experience, and provide a fair, efficient and respectful platform for cleaning workers.
